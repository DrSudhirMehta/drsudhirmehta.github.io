The Big Idea

Great startups aren’t built by lone geniuses in garages — they’re built by small, aligned teams who know how to navigate conflict, make crisp commitments, and keep shipping. Beyond the Garage turns evidence into repeatable practice.

You’ll get diagnostics to spot misalignment early, frameworks to clarify roles and decision rights, and simple rituals that keep momentum without bureaucracy.
